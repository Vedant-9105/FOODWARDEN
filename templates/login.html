2. AI Analysis

3. Get Results

About This Project

The AI Roadside Food Hygiene Checker is an innovative project that combines robotics, AI, and computer vision to make street food safer for everyone. It's designed to automatically detect unhygienic practices, score cleanliness, and spread awareness for better public health. Our mission is simple: empowering safe food choices through technology.
